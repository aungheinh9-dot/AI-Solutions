## Color Usage

### Primary Colors
- **Cyan (#06b6d4)**: Primary accent color used for buttons, links, and highlights
- **Emerald (#10b981)**: Secondary accent color used in gradients and complementary elements
- **Blue (#3b82f6)**: Tertiary accent color used in gradients and special elements

### Background Colors
- **Dark Gray (#111827)**: Main page background
- **Gray (#1f2937)**: Cards, navigation, and secondary backgrounds

### Text Colors
- **White (#FFFFFF)**: Primary text and headings

---

## Typography

The font families used for the overall website was **Inter** due to its high readability. Heading H1-H6 were used accordingly in each page for main titles and subtitles. Normal text was maintained at font-size of **14px** (0.875rem).

### Font Family
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Font Weights**: 300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900
- **Usage**: Applied across all pages for consistent typography

### Heading Hierarchy
- **H1**: Main page titles and hero sections
- **H2**: Section titles and major headings
- **H3**: Subsection titles and card headings
- **H4-H6**: Used for nested content and smaller headings

### Text Sizes
- **Normal Text**: 14px (0.875rem) - `text-sm` in Tailwind CSS
- **Base Text**: 16px (1rem) - `text-base` in Tailwind CSS
- **Small Text**: 12px (0.75rem) - `text-xs` in Tailwind CSS

---

## Buttons

Buttons are designed with the use of rectangular buttons with rounded corners of **8px** (0.5rem). 2 major colors are used for button design: **Cyan** and **White**. These two colors are used together when two buttons are aligned.

### Button Styles
- **Border Radius**: 8px (0.5rem) - `rounded-lg` in Tailwind CSS
- **Shape**: Rectangular with rounded corners
- **Primary Button Color**: Cyan (#06b6d4)
- **Secondary Button Color**: White (#FFFFFF)
- **Gradient Buttons**: Cyan to Emerald gradients for special buttons

### Button Types
1. **Primary Buttons**: Solid Cyan background (#06b6d4) with white text
2. **Secondary Buttons**: Transparent background with Cyan border and Cyan text
3. **Gradient Buttons**: Cyan to Emerald gradient backgrounds
4. **White Buttons**: White background with dark text (used for contrast)

### Button Alignment
When two buttons are aligned side by side, Cyan and White colors are used together to create visual contrast and hierarchy.
